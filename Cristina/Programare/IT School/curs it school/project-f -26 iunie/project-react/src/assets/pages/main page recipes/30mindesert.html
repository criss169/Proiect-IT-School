Quick 30 minutes dessert recipes

Chocolate Mug Cake

Indulge in this rich and moist chocolate mug cake that you can prepare in just 5 minutes. It's perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth cravings!

Berry Parfait

Layer fresh berries, yogurt, and granola to create a delightful and healthy berry parfait. It's a quick and refreshing dessert option for any time of the day.

Strawberry Shortcake

Enjoy the classic combination of fluffy biscuits, fresh strawberries, and whipped cream with this easy strawberry shortcake recipe. It's a crowd-pleaser!

Banana Split Sundae

Take your dessert game to the next level with a scrumptious banana split sundae. Top it with scoops of ice cream, chocolate sauce, nuts, and cherries!

Lava Cake

Indulge in the decadent and irresistible molten lava cake. Break open the chocolatey exterior to reveal a warm, gooey center. It's pure dessert bliss!

Key Lime Pie

Experience the tangy and refreshing flavors of key lime pie. This no-bake dessert comes together quickly and is sure to impress your guests.

Caramel Apple Nachos

Transform fresh apples into a fun and delicious dessert with caramel apple nachos. Drizzle caramel sauce, chocolate, and sprinkle with your favorite toppings.

Mixed Berry Galette

Bake a rustic and mouthwatering mixed berry galette in no time. This free-form pie is filled with a medley of juicy berries and encased in a buttery crust.
