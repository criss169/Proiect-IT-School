Sarmale Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 large cabbage head
  • 500g ground pork
  • 200g rice
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • 500ml vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. Boil the cabbage head in a large pot of water for about 10 minutes. Remove from water, let it cool, and separate the leaves.
  2. In a bowl, mix the ground pork, rice, onion, garlic, tomato paste, thyme, paprika, black pepper, and salt.
  3. Take a cabbage leaf and place a small amount of the pork mixture in the center. Roll the leaf, tucking in the sides, and place it in a large pot.
  4. Repeat the previous step until all the cabbage leaves and pork mixture are used.
  5. Pour the vegetable broth over the sarmale in the pot.
  6. Cover the pot and cook on low heat for about 2 hours.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
