Carbonara Spaghetti Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 150g pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 3 large egg yolks
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 50g grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti until al dente.
  2. In a skillet, cook pancetta or bacon until crispy. Remove from heat and set aside.
  3. In a mixing bowl, whisk together egg yolks, Parmesan cheese, Pecorino Romano cheese, garlic, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Drain the cooked spaghetti, reserving some of the cooking water.
  5. Immediately add the drained spaghetti to the skillet with pancetta. Toss well to combine.
  6. Add the egg mixture to the skillet and quickly toss until the sauce thickens and coats the pasta. If needed, add a little of the reserved cooking water to loosen the sauce.
  7. Serve the carbonara spaghetti hot, garnished with chopped parsley.
