Oreo Cake

Oreo desserts are a popular category of sweet treats that incorporate Oreo cookies as a key ingredient. Oreo cookies are known for their distinctive flavor and texture, featuring a chocolate sandwich cookie with a creamy filling.

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 15 minutes Cook Time: 45 minutes Total Time: 60 minutes Servings: 8
  • Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  • In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  • Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Mix until well combined.
  • Stir in the boiling water. The batter will be thin, but that's okay.
  • Gently fold in the crushed Oreo cookies.
  • Pour the batter evenly into the prepared cake pans.
  • Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  • Remove the cakes from the oven and let them cool in the pans for 10 minutes. Then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
  • Once the cakes are completely cooled, frost them with your favorite chocolate frosting.
  • Decorate the top of the cake with additional Oreo cookies, either whole or crushed.
