1. Chicken curry with rice and fresh cucumbers

Monday Dinner

Chicken curry with rice and fresh cucumbers is a flavorful and aromatic dish that combines tender chicken pieces cooked in a rich and creamy curry sauce, served alongside fluffy rice and refreshing cucumber slices. This recipe brings together the warm and comforting flavors of curry spices with the cool crunch of cucumbers, creating a delightful balance of textures and tastes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb (450g) boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • Salt, to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Cooked rice, for serving
  • Fresh cucumbers, sliced, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan or skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the pan and cook until they are lightly browned on all sides. Make sure the chicken is cooked through and no longer pink in the center.
  3. In a small bowl, combine the curry powder, ground cumin, ground coriander, turmeric powder, and cayenne pepper. Stir the spice mixture into the pan with the chicken and onions, coating the chicken evenly.
  4. Pour in the coconut milk and chicken broth. Stir well to combine all the ingredients. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for about 15-20 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened slightly and the flavors have melded together.
  5. Taste the curry and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper according to your preference.
  6. While the curry is simmering, cook the rice according to the package instructions.
  7. Serve the chicken curry over the cooked rice. Garnish with fresh cucumber slices on the side.

2. Incredible Homemade Calzone

Tuesday Dinner

This recipe teaches you how to make mouthwatering calzones right in your own kitchen. The calzones are filled with your favorite ingredients, such as cooked sausage, pepperoni, vegetables, and gooey melted mozzarella cheese. The homemade dough is soft, pillowy, and perfectly baked to golden perfection. Each bite is a delightful combination of flavors and textures, making it a satisfying and comforting meal that the whole family will enjoy.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your favorite calzone fillings (e.g., cooked sausage, pepperoni, vegetables)

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until the mixture becomes frothy.
  2. Add olive oil, flour, and salt to the yeast mixture. Stir until the dough comes together.
  3. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 5 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a clean cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  5. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C). Punch down the dough and divide it into desired portions.
  6. On a floured surface, roll out each portion into a circle. Spread pizza sauce on one half of each circle.
  7. Add desired fillings and sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese on top.
  8. Fold the other half of the dough over the filling and pinch the edges to seal. Cut small slits on the top to allow steam to escape.
  9. Transfer the calzones onto a baking sheet and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until golden brown.
  10. Remove from the oven and let them cool slightly before serving.

3. Shrimp and Vegetable Skillet

Wednesday Dinner

This delicious skillet dish is packed with a medley of flavors. Succulent shrimp are cooked to perfection and combined with a colorful array of diced onions, bell peppers, garlic, zucchini, and yellow squash.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 bell peppers, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 yellow squash, diced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Add shrimp. Cook until shrimp is pink and cooked through.
  3. Remove shrimp from the skillet and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add onion, bell peppers, and garlic. Sauté until vegetables are tender.
  5. Add zucchini, yellow squash, paprika, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Cook for a few more minutes.
  6. Return the shrimp to the skillet. Stir to combine.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

4. Strawberry Avocado Spinach Salad with Creamy Poppyseed Dressing

Thursday Dinner

This refreshing salad is a perfect balance of flavors and textures. Fresh baby spinach leaves serve as the base, complemented by slices of juicy strawberries, creamy avocado, and tangy red onion. The salad is topped with crumbled feta cheese and sliced almonds for added crunch.It's a vibrant and satisfying salad that is perfect for a light lunch or as a side dish for any meal.

Ingredients:

  • 6 cups baby spinach
  • 1 pint strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/4 cup sliced red onion
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon poppy seeds
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large salad bowl, combine baby spinach, strawberries, avocado, red onion, feta cheese, and sliced almonds.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, Greek yogurt, honey, apple cider vinegar, poppy seeds, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat.
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy!

5. Sausage, Spinach Egg Breakfast Taquitos

Saturday Breakfast

These breakfast taquitos are a delicious way to start your day. They feature a flavorful combination of scrambled eggs, cooked breakfast sausage, fresh baby spinach, and shredded cheddar cheese, all wrapped in a warm flour tortilla.

Ingredients:

  • 8 small flour tortillas
  • 8 eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 pound ground breakfast sausage, cooked and drained
  • 2 cups fresh baby spinach
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil, for frying

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, scramble the eggs over medium heat until cooked through. Remove from heat.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the scrambled eggs, cooked sausage, baby spinach, shredded cheddar cheese, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place a spoonful of the egg mixture onto each flour tortilla. Roll tightly into a taquito shape.
  4. In a separate skillet, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Fry the taquitos until golden brown and crispy.
  5. Remove from heat and let them drain on a paper towel-lined plate.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!

6. Easy London Broil

Sunday Dinner

This recipe takes the classic London broil steak and makes it incredibly easy to prepare. The steak is marinated in a flavorful mixture of olive oil, soy sauce, minced garlic, dried rosemary, dried thyme, black pepper, and salt, resulting in a tender and well-seasoned meat. It's a simple yet impressive dish that is perfect for any occasion, whether it's a casual family dinner or a special gathering.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 pounds London broil steak
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, soy sauce, minced garlic, dried rosemary, dried thyme, black pepper, and salt.
  2. Place the London broil steak in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over it. Turn the steak to coat both sides.
  3. Cover the dish and let the steak marinate in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, or overnight for best results.
  4. Preheat the grill or broiler to medium-high heat.
  5. Remove the steak from the marinade and discard the excess marinade.
  6. Grill or broil the steak for about 5-6 minutes per side, or until it reaches your desired level of doneness.
  7. Transfer the steak to a cutting board and let it rest for a few minutes.
  8. Slice the steak against the grain into thin strips.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy!

7. Cinnabon Cinnamon Roll Cake

Dessert

Indulge your sweet tooth with this delectable cinnamon roll cake inspired by the beloved Cinnabon treats. This cake is a delightful twist on the classic cinnamon rolls, featuring a tender and moist cake base infused with a heavenly cinnamon-sugar swirl.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

    • Instructions:

    1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking pan.
    2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, granulated sugar, baking powder, and salt.
    3. Add milk, melted butter, vanilla extract, and eggs to the dry ingredients. Stir until well combined.
    4. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it evenly.
    5. In a small bowl, mix softened butter, brown sugar, and cinnamon to make the filling. Drop spoonfuls of the filling onto the batter.
    6. Use a knife to swirl the filling into the batter.
    7. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
    8. While the cake is baking, prepare the glaze by whisking powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract together in a small bowl.
    9. Once the cake is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
    10. Drizzle the glaze over the warm cake.
    11. Slice and serve. Enjoy!
