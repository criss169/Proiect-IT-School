Potato Stew

Potato stew is a hearty and comforting dish made with potatoes as the main ingredient. It is a versatile dish that can be prepared in various ways using different ingredients and seasonings. Potato stew is popular in many cuisines around the world, and its preparation can vary based on regional preferences and available ingredients.

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 20 minutes Cook Time: 60 minutes Total Time: 80 minutes Servings: 6
  • Peel and chop the potatoes into small cubes.
  • Dice the onion, mince the garlic, and chop the carrot and celery.
  • In a large pot, heat some oil over medium heat and sauté the onion and garlic until fragrant.
  • Add the carrot and celery and cook for a few minutes.
  • Add the potatoes, vegetable broth, diced tomatoes, thyme, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  • Bring the stew to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for about 20 minutes or until the potatoes are tender.
  • Adjust the seasoning if needed.
  • Serve hot and enjoy!
